WORKING AGREEMENT
-----------------
New durable convention: `Topics/rosetta-stone-ai-node-definitions.md`
holds only settled, current content — a Responsibility or Collaborator
appears there once it's actually part of the definition, not while
still a candidate or under discussion. All correction history, open
questions, un-adopted candidates, and per-node Q&A discovery notes live
in the new companion file,
`Topics/rosetta-stone-ai-node-definitions-qa-log.md`. Bill's framing:
"I'd like to keep the documents I am learning from pristine... [the
Q&A log content is] equally valuable... an external document." This
convention should extend to any future Topics file that accumulates
both reference content and process narrative, not just this one.

Also noted, not yet actionable: Bill's stated intent that `vs-mcp-bridge`
blog posts are a supplement to this reference chain — once a post moves
past repo-only-draft status into the backlog and is actually published,
reference links back to it can be added "as applicable." Recorded as a
note in the new companion file's "Blog capture note" section; no posts
are published yet, so nothing to link today.
